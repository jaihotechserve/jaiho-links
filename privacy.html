Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Jaiho Links (a product of Jaiho Reach, under the Jaiho Digital ecosystem), we respect your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Because Jaiho Links operates as a managed static website service, we collect minimal data:

  • Client Information: Business names, public contact details, social media URLs, and brand assets provided to us directly by clients to construct their profiles.
  • Usage Data: Basic, anonymized telemetry (such as IP addresses and browser types via standard edge network logs) to ensure system security and optimize load times.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used strictly to:

  • Create, host, and maintain your digital link profile.
  • Provide customer support and administrative communications.
  • Ensure network security and prevent abuse of the Jaiho ecosystem.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal identification information to others. The links and information you provide to us for your profile are inherently designed to be public. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers (like Vercel for hosting) solely for operating our website.

4. Security

We implement robust, industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All Jaiho Links profiles are served over encrypted HTTPS connections. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Links

Jaiho Links profiles consist of links to third-party websites. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.
